About us

Cash4CropTM is an online agricultural marketing platform that caters for South African farmers and consumers. The platform facilitates the marketing of agricultural commodity groups such as Vegetables, Grain crops and Livestock through an online platform with a greater reach.

This platform enables farmers to maximise their profit margins by cutting out broker fees and transport costs to the formal market. By cutting out the middle man, Cash4CropTM allows farmers to set prices as they see fit. Cash4CropTM does not act as a trader or agent, nor does it have a vested interest in promoting specific deals.

The online platform allows the buyer to interact directly with the seller, and payments between the buyer and the seller are made offline. The aim of this platform is to help emerging farmers and rural-based businesses to tap into new markets and become active participants in the country’s economy.
